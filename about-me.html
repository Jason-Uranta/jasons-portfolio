About Me

Hi! I'm Jason Uranta, a software engineer and designer at Brown University. I am always trying to learn new things from design to data analysis to AI architecture. During my time at Brown, I’ve been a mentor in the club MOSAIC+ for minorities in CS, gone to conferences, and broadened my knowledge as a Thrive Scholar.

I believe that “a jack of all trades is a master of none, but oftentimes better than a master of one” and try to apply that to all aspects of life — from programming languages to new recipes to adapting in a new city.

In my free time, I love to travel, try out new restaurants, and have board game nights with friends. I’m a lover of all Netflix shows — action-packed, dramatic, and romantic — and a strong advocate for cinnamon sweet pretzels at the movies.
